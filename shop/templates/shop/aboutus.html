Our Story

Preserving Bangladesh's rich heritage through authentic handcrafted products

Our Mission

At Lokoj Crafts, we're dedicated to preserving Bangladesh's traditional craftsmanship while providing sustainable livelihoods for rural artisans. We bridge the gap between skilled artisans and conscious consumers worldwide.

Our Vision

We envision a world where traditional crafts thrive, artisans earn fair wages, and consumers appreciate the stories behind handmade products. Our goal is to become Bangladesh's most trusted platform for authentic handicrafts.

How We Started

Founded in 2025, Lokoj Crafts began as a small initiative to help a group of bamboo weavers in rural Bangladesh. After witnessing the decline of traditional crafts due to mass production, our founders created this platform to connect artisans directly with global customers.

Today, we work with over 200 artisans across 15 craft clusters in Bangladesh, helping preserve techniques passed down through generations.

Our Core Values

Fair Trade

We ensure artisans receive fair compensation for their work, with transparent pricing that benefits both makers and buyers.

Sustainability

All our products use natural, eco-friendly materials and traditional techniques that minimize environmental impact.

Quality Craftsmanship

Each product is meticulously handcrafted using time-honored techniques, ensuring exceptional quality and durability.
